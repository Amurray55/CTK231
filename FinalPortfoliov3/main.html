Anthony Murray

Anthony is a professional Circus artist that is dabbling in other artistic areas such as website design, digital painting and videogame design. He just enjoys making cool stuff and wants to share that with anyone that would like to see it. In late 2013 Anthony started his circus journey while attending Illinois State University, joining the student organization the Gamma Phi Circus. After spending 3 years there and finishing up his Bachelor's degree, he made is way to Tennesee to perform in a few circus dinner shows for Dolly Parton. During his circus career he mostly focused on group/ensemble acts such as teeterboard, russian bar and acrosport/adagio. Most recently Anthony is working on a few solo acts revolving around aerial straps and juggling.
